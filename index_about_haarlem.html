ABOUT HAARLEM

WHY NAVIGATE IN HAARLEM?

Haarlem is considered a good city to navigate by boat on the canals for several reasons:

Scenic Canals:

Haarlem features picturesque canals that wind through the city, lined with historic buildings and charming scenery. Navigating these canals provides a visually pleasing experience, with opportunities to enjoy the city's architecture from a unique perspective.

Historical and Architectural Attractions:

The canals in Haarlem pass by many historical and architectural attractions. Boating allows visitors to access and appreciate landmarks such as Teylers Museum, and other notable attractions that are mentioned on the Attractions In the Route page.

Relaxing Atmosphere:

Boating on the canals of Haarlem provides a relaxing and leisurely experience. The calm waters and scenic surroundings create a peaceful atmosphere, making it an enjoyable way to explore the city at a more leisurely pace.

Access to Hidden Gems:

Some parts of Haarlem may not be as easily accessible by foot, but the canals provide a unique vantage point and access to hidden gems. Exploring the city by boat allows visitors to discover charming corners, local cafes, and lesser-known attractions.

Tourist and Local Appeal:

Boating is popular among both tourists and locals in Haarlem. The canals serve as a hub for various water activities, including boat tours and rentals. This creates a vibrant atmosphere where people can enjoy the water, socialize, and explore the city's waterways together.
