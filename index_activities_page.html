ACTIVITIES IN BOATS

WHAT ARE PEOPLE DOING ON THE BOATS?

Parties

Haarlem hosts events and festivals that take advantage of its canals. These can include boat parades, water-themed celebrations, and other festivities that add to the vibrancy of the city's waterways.

Restaurants

Some boat companies offer dining program where you can enjoy a meal while cruising along the canals. It's a delightful way to experience the city's charm.

Meetings

Enjoy downtime during boat excursions for relaxation, incorporating leisure activities or combine relaxation and business discussions by hosting comfortable meetings on boats, offering a sophisticated and enjoyable setting.
