NAVIGATION IN HAARLEM

The Netherlands is known for its extensive canal systems and the popularity of boating activities for geographic and management reasons because a significant portion of the Netherlands is situated below sea level and the country has a complex system of water management. The need to control water levels and prevent flooding has led to the creation of an intricate network of canals. The canals have become a major tourist attraction. Boat tours along the canals offer visitors a unique way to explore the historic cities of the Netherlands.
